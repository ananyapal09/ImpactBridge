*Impact Bridge* is a web platform designed to enhance transparency and trust in online donations. By providing real-time tracking, instant verification, and AI-driven donor-NGO matching, Impact Bridge ensures donors can see the tangible impact of their contributions while supporting verified, cause-driven organizations.

---

## Problem Statement

The absence of transparency and verifiable impact in online donations often creates mistrust between donors and NGOs. Donors lack visibility into how funds are utilized, and NGOs struggle to demonstrate accountability.

---

## Solution

Impact Bridge addresses these challenges through:

- *Verified NGOs*: Onboarding only legitimate, cause-driven organizations.  
- *Live Tracking*: Turning donations into visible, real-time impact.  
- *Instant Verification*: Donors receive shareable Impact Certificates immediately.  
- *Zero-Trust Giving*: Eliminating uncertainty about fund usage.  
- *Radical Transparency*: Providing real-time impact tracking for all donations.  
- *AI-Driven Matching*: Intelligent mapping of donors to NGOs based on interests.

---

## Tech Stack

- *Frontend*: React.js, Tailwind CSS  
- *Backend*: Node.js, Express.js  
- *Database*: MongoDB

---

## Features / Use Cases

- Donors can see real-time donation progress, building trust.  
- Donors can track the impact of their contributions in real time.  
- Donors receive detailed information on how their donations are used.  
- Donors view live updates reflecting the impact of their contributions.
